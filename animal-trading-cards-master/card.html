Pikachu

a lightning type pokemon

  • category: mouse
  • length: 1'04"
  • life span: 20 - 40 years
  • habitat: forests and woodlands

pikachu (japanese: ピカチュウ) is an electric-type pokémon. it evolves from pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into raichu when exposed to a thunder stone.
